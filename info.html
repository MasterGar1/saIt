Exciting chess rules

How to play

In the game you have the same starting setup as a normal chess game, but the difference is the behaviour of the pieces. The game is played by two players - white and black. You can only do one action per turn - Attack or Move

Each piece and what it does

  • Pawn - A pawn has a small amount of HP and a weak attack. It strikes a figure in the 3 tiles ahead of itself. After attacking, it gains 1 damage protection for the next time it gets hit. The pawn can only move by one tile at a time in each direction.
  • Knight - knights are very mobile. They do not have separate movement and attack patterns. When they move over a target, they strike them. Their HP and damage are average. They can jump up to three tiles ahead of themselves.
  • Rook - the rooks are very tanky and devastating amounts of damage, but are unable to move. They can only remain in their starting position and attack in a square range of 4 tiles around them, but every other round.
  • Bishop - the bighop is a very agressive piece. It does high amounts of damage to all targets it the three tiles in front, but has very low HP. Bishops can swiftly move two tiles in each direction.
  • Queen - the queen, as all women, is a very deadly figure. It can move long distances and strike foes with critical strikes which occur every other hit she deals. She has an average amount of HP and medium attack power, but can deal double damage with critical strikes.
  • King - the king is the ruler of the nation. He cannot attack, but can heal allied units within the eight surrounding tiles. He has a high amount of health and can move one tile at a time.
